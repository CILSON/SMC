# <u>HH/HL & LL/LH</u>

A "Higher High" occurs when previous High is broken or price of the assest reaches High which higher than previous High, without being preceded by Lower Low.

A "Lower High" occurs when New Low is made above the previous Low, without being preceded by Lower Low. \n

A "Lower Low" occurs when previous Low is broken or price of the assest reaches Low which lower than previous Low, without being preceded by Higher High. \n

A "Lower High" occurs when New High is made above the previous High, without being preceded by Higher High. \n
